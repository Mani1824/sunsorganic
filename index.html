Yes, We Cost More. Here’s Exactly Why.

Most oils are refined, chemically processed, and deodorised. Ours are not. Cold pressing is slower, yields less oil per seed, and requires premium raw material — but preserves everything nature put in there.

❌ Cheap Refined Oils
🔥Solvent-extracted with hexane chemicals
🔥Heated to 200°C+ destroying all nutrients
🔥Bleached and deodorised to mask quality
🔥Preservatives and additives added
🔥Yields 99% from low-quality seeds
🔥No traceability to origin farm

✅ Sunsa Pure Cold Pressed
✨Mechanical press only — zero chemicals

✨Natural colour & aroma — nothing to hide
✨Zero additives, zero preservatives

Zero Chemicals

🌿
Full Nutrition
Retains natural flavour, aroma, and 100% of the nutritional profile — omega-3s, vitamin E, polyphenols.

Better for Health
No trans-fats, no additives. Pure cold pressed oils support heart health, immunity and overall wellness.
