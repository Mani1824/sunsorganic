How It's Made

From Seed to Bottle

A slow, traditional process that keeps every drop pure and full of nutrition.

01
🌾

Premium Seeds

We source top-grade seeds directly from trusted single-origin farms across India.

02
⚙️

Wooden Cold Press

Seeds are slowly pressed in a traditional wooden press, never exceeding 40°C.

03
💧

Natural Settling

Oil is naturally settled and lightly filtered — no bleaching, no deodorising, no chemicals.

04
🫙

Fresh Bottling

Every batch is lab tested, then bottled fresh to lock in aroma, colour and nutrition.

The Difference

Why Cold Pressed?

Refined oils are heated, bleached and chemically treated. Ours never are.

🌡️

No Heat, No Damage

Cold pressing uses no heat, preserving vitamins, antioxidants and fatty acids that refining destroys.

🚫

Zero Chemicals

No hexane, no solvents, no bleaching — just pure mechanical pressing as nature intended.

🌿

Full Nutrition

Retains natural flavour, aroma and 100% of the nutritional profile — Omega-3s, Vitamin E, polyphenols.

🫀

Better for Health

No trans-fats, no additives. Pure cold pressed oils support heart health, immunity and wellness.
